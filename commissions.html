Commissions

Custom artwork created for you

Commission a one-of-a-kind painting for your home, office, or as a meaningful gift. Every commission begins with a conversation about your vision and ends with a signed original delivered to your door.

1

Inquiry

Fill out the commission form with your vision, preferred size, colors, and any reference photos.

2

Concept

We discuss your vision in detail. I provide preliminary sketches and a detailed quote.

3

Deposit

A 50% deposit secures your place in my schedule. Materials preparation begins.

4

Creation

Your painting is created in the studio with progress photos shared at key milestones.

5

Review

High-resolution photos sent for your approval. Minor adjustments included.

6

Delivery

Final payment, professional crating, certificate of authenticity, and shipping.

What’s Included

  • Initial consultation and concept discussion
  • Preliminary sketches
  • Progress photos during creation
  • Minor revisions at completion
  • Professional varnishing
  • Signed certificate of authenticity
  • Professional crating for shipping
  • Ready-to-hang hardware
